About Me


Hi, my name is Andrea! I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a degree in History and a certificate in Health Informatics. I am currently employed at Austin Regional Clinc as an Epic EMR Specialist. In this role, I provide Epic Ambulatory support to the 24 Austin Regional Clinics, train new providers, and participate in system upgrades through analysis and testing to support new workflows and procedures.

Outside of work, I like to go hiking with my dog, trying new donut places around Austin, and seeing as many new movies with MoviePass before it eventually goes out of business. I am interested in learning to code, and have enrolled in the UT Coding Boot Camp with the goal of finding a developing job after graduation. I am excited about this new opportunity and seeing how much I can learn over the next six months.
